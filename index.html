Previous Work

PhD research

Research project into the mechanisms behind the behaviour of fluorescent DNA base analogues, used to investigate structural changes and flexibility of DNA in its natural environment in real time. Fluorescence spectroscopy, especially time-resolved fluorescence decay measurements using a femtosecond Ti:sapphire laser light source, was used to show that the fluorescence output of DNA base analogues in DNA is intrinsically linked to the wider DNA structure.

Property Prices in Wales

A short project looking at prices paid for properties in Wales from data accessible through the UK Land Registry. Python code in a Jupyter notebook was used to cluster property sales by price. It was found these price clusters were also clustered geographically, in particular there was a sharp difference in the mean price of rural properties compared to urban properties.
